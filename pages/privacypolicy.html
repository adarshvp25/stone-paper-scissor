Privacy Policy

At RPS Royale, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Anonymous Gameplay Data: We may collect anonymous data about your choices and gameplay for improving the experience.
  • Optional Login Information: If you choose to sign up, we collect your email and username to personalize your experience and save your scores.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to improve game functionality, track leaderboards, and offer a seamless gaming experience across devices.

3. Data Protection

Your data is securely stored and never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

4. Cookies

We may use cookies to enhance your experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. Contact us at support@rpsroyale.com for any data-related requests.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
