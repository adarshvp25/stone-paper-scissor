How to Play

  1. Choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors: Make your move by clicking one of the three icons.
  2. Your Opponent Will Be Selected: A real player or bot will face you in a head-to-head match.
  3. Game Rules: Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beat Paper, and Paper beats Rock.
  4. Score the Highest and Win: Play multiple rounds, improve your strategy, and climb the leaderboard!
